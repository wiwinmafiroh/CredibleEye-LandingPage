List of Hoax Detection Tips

01

Verify Source's Credibility

Check before trusting or sharing. Look for reputable news outlets and trusted websites.

02

Examine the Headline

Analyze headlines for sensationalism or clickbait language. Genuine news headlines are usually clear and factual.

03

Scrutinize Images

Use reverse image search to determine authenticity. Be cautious of misleading or manipulated visuals.

04

Cross-Check Multiple Sources

Compare information from multiple reliable sources to validate its accuracy.

05

Watch for Biased Language

Be wary of content that contains extreme bias or overly emotional language.

06

Check Fact-Checking Sites

Utilize fact-checking websites to verify the accuracy of claims and debunk hoaxes.
